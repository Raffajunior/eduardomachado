About

A musician
between two worlds

Born in Brazil and based in Geneva for many years, Eduardo Machado is a musician deeply inspired by the richness of guitar traditions across cultures and historical periods.

“The guitar is both a bridge between civilizations and a space of absolute freedom.”

Trained in classical guitar, renaissance lute and baroque guitar, he enriches his teaching with deep knowledge of Brazilian music and historically informed performance practices. His lessons welcome beginners as well as advanced musicians who want to refine their technique or explore new repertoires.
